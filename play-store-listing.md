## Full description (max 4000 chars)

Invoice & PDF Generator is built for Australian sole traders and small trade businesses — earthmovers, graders, bobcat operators, and construction crews who need to track hours, log machine hire, and send professional invoices without the office admin headache.

**Track your day automatically**
The app uses GPS geofencing to detect when you arrive at and leave a job site. The timer starts and stops automatically — even if the app is closed — so you never forget to log your hours. Worksites are saved with adjustable detection radius (50–500m) to suit tight yards or wide paddocks.

**Built for the way tradies actually work**
- Standard labour rate (default $55/hr, fully adjustable)
- Extra labourer rate ($30/hr)
- Machine hire: Excavator, Grader, Bobcat, Dozer, Tractor — all with individual hourly rates
- Materials tracking with a quick-select store
- GST toggle — include or exclude 10% GST per invoice
- Multiple clients, selectable per job

**Generate professional PDF invoices in seconds**
When the job's done, hit Invoice. Your hours, machines, materials, and GST are totalled automatically. Generate a polished PDF and share it directly from your phone via email, SMS, or WhatsApp. No computer needed.

**Team timesheets built in**
Running a crew? Employees can log their own hours on their phones, and those hours sync directly into your invoice under a Team Labour section. Link up with a simple 6-character business code — no complicated setup.

**Your data, always safe**
All records sync to the cloud via Firebase so you never lose a day's work, even if you change phones. Works offline too — everything keeps running without signal and syncs when you're back in range.

**No subscriptions. No lock-in.**
Pay once, own it. No monthly fees, no per-invoice charges, no upsells.
